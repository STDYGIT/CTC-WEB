CTC: Crack The Code

Unleash Your Coding Potential in a two-phase event combining a Coding Contest and Hackathon.

Event Details

CTC: Crack The Code is designed to promote a coding culture, skill development, and real-world problem-solving. Participate in a two-phase event with:

  • Elimination Round: Coding Contest hosted by GeeksforGeeks.
  • Final Round: Hackathon hosted by CodeChef.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Problem-Solving Skills: Accuracy, efficiency, and optimization of solutions.
  • Innovation: Creativity and originality, especially in the hackathon round.
  • Code Quality: Readability, structure, and maintainability.
  • Collaboration & Presentation: Effective communication and presentation of solutions.

Certifications & Prizes

  • Certificates: Participation, Winner, and Excellence certificates for outstanding performances.
  • GeeksforGeeks: Rs. 2000 discount coupon for winners; Rs. 300 for all participants. Tech gadgets for top 3 and T-shirts for next 7 performers.
  • CodeChef: Pro Subscription for the winner; 40% discount for 2nd and 3rd places; 30% discount for all participants.

Our Partners & Sponsors

  • GeeksforGeeks: Platform partner for Elimination Round.
  • CodeChef: Platform partner for Final Round, with marketing and social media collaboration.
